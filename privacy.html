Privacy Policy

Grocery & Meal Planner · Last updated 9 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Grocery & Meal Planner app (“the app”, “we”) collects, how it is used, and your choices. The app is a personal grocery checklist, weekly meal planner and shopping-cost tracker.

Information we collect

  • Account: your email address, used only to sign you in and identify your account. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google to create your account.
  • Your content: the grocery items, sections, meal plan, shopping history (including any bill totals you choose to enter), and any bill/receipt photos you add. This is stored so it syncs across your devices and with anyone you share your list with via a share code.

Receipt scanning

If you add a bill photo and use the scan feature, that image is sent to OpenAI’s vision API to read the items on the receipt and return them as text. Under OpenAI’s API terms, data sent through the API is not used to train their models. We only use the returned items to build your shopping list.

How your data is stored

Accounts and content are stored securely using Supabase (our database and authentication provider). Access is protected so that only you (and people you share your list with) can read or change your list.

What we don’t do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not show ads or use third-party advertising trackers.
  • We do not collect location, contacts, or unrelated device data.

Your choices & deletion

You can remove individual items, photos, and shopping history in the app at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, email us at the address below and we will remove it.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy; the “last updated” date above will change accordingly.
